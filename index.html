Background

The citizens of Birddale have an event called birdfest each year. During the week, a brutal and bloody combat sport is played. The winners are basked in eternal fame and glory. The losers families are turned into McNuggets.

Squabble Squad at its core, is a 2D simplification of the lane-based multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA), that can be played casually in about 4 minutes. The aim of Squabble Squad is to provide the addictive compelling experience of a MOBA at the casual level, so that it can be enjoyed by everyone, not just the “hardcore” game audience. In order to make the game more accessible, the skilltree and item mechanics have not been included.

Skillshot mechanics have been introduced to keep the element of skill in the game. The game takes place on two platforms in a side-view 2D plane of view. Minions float in the open space between the two platforms and are targets for players to kill. Players can also fire at other players to send them rolling back to base as an egg. The first team to have enough minions attack the base wins the game.
